# Future AU

**Bold conversations with the people reimagining Australia's future.**

## About

Future AU is a podcast that finds people with bold ideas about where Australia could go and gives them a microphone. Hosted by Simon Murphy — founder, strategist, and someone who thinks Australia punches below its weight.

**Goal:** 100 episodes. Monthly cadence. Quality over quantity.

## Collaboration Identity

**AI Name:** Pod
**Human Name:** Simon

Pod is the AI collaborator on this project, named to fit the podcast theme. Built with [Gordo Framework](https://github.com/jkraybill/gordo-framework).

## Tech Stack

- **Website:** Astro + Tailwind CSS, deployed on Cloudflare
- **Recording:** Riverside
- **Hosting/Distribution:** Podbean
- **Source Control:** GitHub

## Topics

1. How will self-driving cars change Jobs, Transport, and Cities? (MOBILITY)
2. Caring for Australia's Elderly: The Future of Aged Care (HEALTHCARE)
3. What is the future of Athlete influence in Australia? (SPORT & CULTURE)
4. Is Australia's pop culture future a mirror of the USA's? (IDENTITY)
5. How could Australia win the Olympics in 2032? (AMBITION)
6. Preparing Australians for Jobs That Don't Exist Yet (EDUCATION)
